Every Small Step Counts Whether You Believe it or Not

By Jennifer Dinh

Published on October 16th, 2022

Are you feeling as if you aren’t learning anything, not getting out of your comfort zone enough, not reaching your goals you wanted to? Let me tell you that you are. As someone who has their fair share of self doubts and worries, I know for a fact that everyone is making progress one way or another when they put their minds to it.
As long as you are trying through putting effort, even if you don’t see a drastic result right now doesn’t mean you won’t get there. It’s difficult to shift your mindset into that type of way.
Here are a few steps you can take to help yourself understand every minimal growth means so much:

  • Being proactive
  • Have a positive mindset in believing in yourself and avoid others opinions

Further more about being proactive, it’s different from saying one thing and actually putting action into something and doing it. To say something but never follow through means you aren’t actually trying to do something about whatever your end goal or situation is. If you are being proactive whether it be changing something small in your daily life such as writing down what you are going to do for that day or even pushing yourself to interact with one new person everyday.
Furthermore about having a positive mindset and avoiding others opinions, I know thats more difficult to say than actually believing, but it’s how you will succeed. If there are haters who believe you aren't making progress, you just have to push through and understand that they will never know 100% how much you have tried to put into your goal. No one will ever believe in you as much as yourself as you are the most important person that needs to believe in yourself.

Learn How to Say No

By Jennifer Dinh

Published on October 16th, 2022

Do you ever have a homework assignment, upcoming exams or you are just plain busy but you aren’t unsure how to say no when someone asks you to do things?
I know I’ve been in that boat before so tune in to hear more on how to say no but in a way that your friends will understand.
Here are the few steps you can take to ensure that you are able to say no to situations:

  • Be Firm with Your Answer
  • Be Sure to Thank Them, Be Gentle, and Explain the Situation

Furthermore about being firm with your answer, To be firm but kind is quite difficult to do for some as I know it’s easy to get frustrated when your friends keep pestering you to go here and there when you already said no the first time. You have to understand that it’s okay to say no to plans as you are the only person that knows your limits on how much you can handle! You don’t want to ever get overwhelmed :).
Furthermore about being sure to thank them and explaining the situation, You have to be sure to not be harsh or be rude to others when you are turning them down as it might make them more upset or disappointed. It can get frustrating when you are trying to tell them that you truly can’t and they keep asking you to, but it’s important to explain the situation to them so they would be more understanding.
